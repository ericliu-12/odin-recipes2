Alaska's Butter-basted Steak

Description

  • 2 cups cooked white rice (preferably chilled)
  • 1/2 pound (about 225g) medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn), thawed if frozen
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the shrimp and cook until pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes. Remove shrimp from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the remaining tablespoon of oil. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add mixed vegetables to the pan and stir-fry until they are tender-crisp.
  5. Push the vegetables to the side of the pan and pour the beaten eggs into the empty space. Scramble the eggs until cooked through.
  6. Add the cooked rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps and mixing it with the vegetables and eggs.
  7. Return the cooked shrimp to the pan.
  8. Drizzle soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil over the rice. Stir well to combine and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through.
  10. Garnish with sliced green onions and serve hot.
